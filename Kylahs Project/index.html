Orenda Originals is a small business owned by a 17 year old girl name Kylah Covey. Kylah originally started the business in 2020; However, making its first debut to people on Febuary 24th, 2021. She and her business are based in a small town called Irvine, Ky. This small businesss was brought about by Kylah to create pieces that can express any style, aesthetic, person, or memory. The main goal for this business was to keep everything very affordable but with quality. She did not want to become "big" or "known" for her busiess, but instead she knew everyone cannot afford expensive jewllery, therefore, she wanted to create something that anyone can experience. All of the jewllery is hand made and pieced together by Kylah. She is an aspiring entrepreneur that believes in a businesss that is able to make anyone and everyone feel so beautiful and confident! Orenda Originals is a great gift for any holiday, anniversary, birthday, etc.
